Dieter Rams pioneered civilized, honest, and timeless design. He urges us to ask 100 year questions and always defer to the self expression of people and their complex and fascinating lives.

Steve Jobs showed us that technology can change us and make us more than we are. He left us with Apple, a place where imagination is nurtured, applied, and executed in the most creative ways.

Jony Ive values the merit of ideas above all else, and through a creative process of countless iteration, produced a market-wide orientation toward simplicity, clarity, and incredible delight.

Camille Paglia uses her taste for great art to provoke and widen the mind. She asserts her street smart, Amazon individualism with fascinating rigor and flexibility granted only by her cosmic vision.
