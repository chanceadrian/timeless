Emotional Interaction

Humans form emotional connections to physical objects.

We handle items with care, or sometimes throw them in a fit of rage.

In Shelf, it’s no different.

There’s emotional interaction.

Built for every emotion, even anger.
Try pushing over a shelf and watch as your items fall to the ground. Reorganize them with just your eyes, or sit on the floor and go through things one by one — just stomp or throw something to remove it.
